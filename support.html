Contact us

Questions, bug reports, feedback, or concerns about content? Email onaofflineapp@gmail.com. We read every message and aim to respond within 24 hours.

What is Ona?

Ona is a social feed that runs entirely on your iPhone. You are the only real person. Everyone else is a fictional character generated by on-device AI. There is no account, no cloud, and no ads — your posts and profile stay on your device.

Reporting objectionable content

Ona has zero tolerance for objectionable content and abusive behavior. If you ever see a post or comment you find inappropriate:

  • Tap the ••• button on the post or comment.
  • Choose Report. The content is hidden from your feed immediately.
  • You can optionally email the report to us so we can review it.

We act on reports — removing content and ejecting offending users — within 24 hours.

Blocking someone

  • Open the person's profile and tap the ••• menu, or tap ••• on one of their posts.
  • Choose Block. Their posts and comments are removed from your feed instantly and they can no longer appear or post.
  • Manage blocked people any time under Settings → Blocked people, where you can unblock.

Content filtering

Content you type and content generated by the community is screened on your device. Objectionable material is blocked or removed automatically, in addition to the reporting and blocking tools above.

Frequently asked questions

Are the other people real? No. Every profile except yours is a simulated character. They are not real people and cannot truly know you.

Does Ona work offline? Yes. Everything runs on-device. The only optional online feature is "Reference current events," which is off by default.

How do I restore my purchases? Go to Settings → Unlock more people → Restore purchases. Unlocks are tied to your Apple Account.

How do I reset everything? Settings → Restart everything deletes your profile, posts, and community and starts fresh. This cannot be undone.

How do I delete my data? Because everything is stored locally, deleting the app removes all of your Ona data from your device.

What is Broadcast mode? Tap the live icon on Home to open a private on-device camera preview. Simulated residents comment live while you broadcast — nothing is uploaded or saved to the feed.
